Courses we offer

At Arena Animation GTB Nagar, we offer a wide range of creative and professional courses designed to help students build successful careers in the media, design, and technology industries. Our programs are created according to current industry trends and focus on both practical learning and creative development. Whether you are a beginner starting your creative journey or a student looking to improve professional skills, our courses provide the right balance of theory, practical projects, and real-world experience. Students get hands-on training with industry-standard software, portfolio-building guidance, workshops, presentations, and live assignments that prepare them for future opportunities. We provide degree programs, diploma courses, and short-term certification courses in areas like animation, VFX, graphic design, UI/UX, web development, video editing, sketching, and multimedia. Our experienced faculty members guide students at every step and help them improve creativity, technical knowledge, and confidence. With career-focused training and placement assistance, our courses are designed to make students industry-ready and help them turn their passion into a successful profession.
