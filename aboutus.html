ABOUT US

Arena Animation GTB Nagar is one of the leading creative education institutes dedicated to shaping the future of students in the fields of animation, graphic design, VFX, UI/UX design, web development, video editing, and multimedia. With a strong focus on practical learning and industry-oriented training, the institute helps students develop both creative and technical skills required to succeed in today’s digital and media industry. The center provides modern classrooms, advanced software training, experienced faculty members, and hands-on projects that give students real-world exposure and confidence. From beginners to aspiring professionals, Arena Animation GTB Nagar creates a learning environment where students can explore their creativity and build a strong foundation for their careers.

The institute believes that learning goes beyond classrooms and software training. To help students improve communication skills, confidence, and presentation abilities, monthly presentation sessions are organized where students get opportunities to showcase their work and creativity. Arena Animation GTB Nagar also conducts mock interviews, workshops, seminars, and creative activities to prepare students for professional environments. The institute values student feedback and regularly takes reviews to continuously improve the learning experience. Special initiatives like “Artist of the Month” motivate students by recognizing creativity, talent, and consistent performance, while also helping them strengthen their portfolios for future career opportunities.

Arena Animation GTB Nagar offers degree programs, diploma courses, and short-term certification courses designed according to current industry trends and demands. The institute focuses on portfolio development, live projects, and practical assignments that help students become job-ready and stand out professionally. With career guidance and placement assistance, students are prepared for opportunities in industries such as animation, advertising, film production, gaming, digital marketing, web design, and media entertainment. By combining creativity, technology, and practical knowledge, Arena Animation GTB Nagar continues to inspire students to turn their passion into a successful and rewarding career.

OUR HISTORY

Arena Animation was established in 1996 as the training and education division of Aptech Limited. It was created with the vision of providing professional education in animation, multimedia, and digital arts at a time when the media and entertainment industry in India was rapidly growing. Arena Animation became one of the first institutes in India to introduce structured training programs in animation and creative technologies, helping students build careers in the digital and entertainment world.

Over the years, Arena Animation expanded its courses from animation and multimedia to include VFX, graphic design, UI/UX design, web development, gaming, video editing, and digital content creation. With industry-focused training, practical learning methods, and modern software education, the institute gained popularity among students who wanted creative career opportunities. Arena Animation also introduced live projects, portfolio-building activities, workshops, and placement support to make students industry-ready.

Today, Arena Animation has a strong presence with hundreds of centers across India and in several countries around the world. It has trained thousands of students who are now working in industries such as film production, advertising, television, gaming, web design, and digital media. With nearly 30 years of experience, Arena Animation continues to be one of the leading institutes for creative and multimedia education, helping students turn their creativity into successful professional careers.
