OUR FACULTY

At Arena Animation GTB Nagar, our faculty members are experienced, creative, and dedicated to helping students grow in the field of design and multimedia. They focus on practical learning and guide students through every step of their creative journey. From classroom training to live project support, our mentors ensure that students gain both technical knowledge and industry-level skills. The faculty regularly organizes presentations, portfolio reviews, and mock interviews to build students’ confidence and communication skills. They encourage creativity, innovation, and teamwork in every project. With friendly guidance and hands-on training, students get the perfect environment to learn and improve. Our teachers stay updated with the latest industry trends and software to provide modern and professional training. Whether students are beginners or experienced learners, the faculty helps them develop strong creative and technical abilities for successful careers.
