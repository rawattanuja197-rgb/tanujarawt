Short-Term Courses

Graphic Designing

Learn how to create posters, logos, banners, social media posts, and brand designs using professional software like Photoshop and Illustrator. This course helps students improve creativity and visual communication skills.

Sketching and Digital Illustration

Understand the basics of drawing, character sketching, shading, and digital art creation. Students learn how to turn creative ideas into professional illustrations.

Learn video cutting, transitions, effects, color correction, and audio editing using industry-standard editing software. This course helps students create professional videos for YouTube, films, and social media.

UI/UX Designing

Understand how to design user-friendly mobile apps and websites with attractive layouts and smooth user experiences. Students learn wireframing, prototyping, and app interface designing.

Motion Graphics

Learn to create animated text, visual effects, and creative motion videos used in advertisements, films, and digital content creation.

Social Media Design

Learn how to design engaging content for Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and other social media platforms using mordern design techniques and trends.
